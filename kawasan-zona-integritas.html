Kawasan Zona Integritas RSUD Gambiran Kota Kediri siap menuju WBK & WBBM

View this site in: EN | ID | JP

A hospital is a healthcare institution that provides comprehensive individual healthcare services, including inpatient care, outpatient care, and emergency care. (Ministry of Health Regulation No. 72 of 2016)

Gambiran Regional General Hospital in Kediri City is one of the work units implementing the Integrity Zone towards a Corruption-Free Zone (WBK) and Clean and Serving Bureaucracy Zone (WBBM) within the Kediri City Government, based on the Decree of the Mayor of Kediri Number: 188.45/ 206 / 419.033 / 2022.

The Integrity Zone (ZI) is a title given to government agencies whose leaders and staff are committed to realizing a Corruption-Free Zone (WBK)/Clean and Serving Bureaucracy Zone (WBBM) through bureaucratic reform, particularly in terms of preventing corruption and improving the quality of public services. (Permenpan 52 of 2014).
