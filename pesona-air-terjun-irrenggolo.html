Pesona Air Terjun Irrenggolo - Kediri Punya Cerita

Kediri, East Java, has several very interesting tourist destinations, one of which is Irenggolo Waterfall, located in Besuki Hamlet, Jugo Village, Mojo District. Irenggolo Waterfall is the perfect tourist destination to spend your weekend with family, friends, or companions.
